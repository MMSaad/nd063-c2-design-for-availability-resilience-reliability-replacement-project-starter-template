Minimum RTO for a single AZ outage
The needed time to promote read replica within same region
AWS documentation : This is determined by many factors within your architecture, such as the size of your database

Minimum RTO for a single region outage
The needed time to promote read replica in the secondary region
AWS documentation : This is determined by many factors within your architecture, such as the size of your database, the distance between the two Regions
